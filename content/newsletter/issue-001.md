**DOJ Expands Health Care for Federal Prisoners ‚Äî $47.8M to Heritage Health Solutions**

The U.S. Marshals Service just awarded a $47.8M contract to Heritage Health Solutions for nationwide prisoner healthcare delivery. This isn't a new concept ‚Äî but the scale signals a broader trend: federal agencies are increasingly outsourcing complex healthcare operations to specialized vendors.

If you're in healthcare services, correctional health, or managed care, USMS is actively buying. The incumbent (Heritage) has held this contract since October 2025, but recompetes are coming.

**Why it matters:** Healthcare services contracts across DOJ, DHS, and VA represent a growing $5B+ annual market. Agencies need specialized providers, and the barrier to entry is lower than you'd think for firms with clinical capacity.
